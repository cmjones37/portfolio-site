About Me

Shipping products that matter

A brief look at who I am, where I've been, and how I think about product.

I'm a Product Manager with a deep focus on AI integration and end-to-end product development. I believe great products come from obsessive user empathy, clear metrics, and the courage to cut.

My background spans 0→1 product builds, cross-functional team leadership, and data-informed roadmapping. I've worked across SaaS, consumer apps, and AI-native tools — always with an eye on what actually moves the needle.

Outside of work, I write about the intersection of AI and product strategy, contribute to open-source tooling, and mentor aspiring PMs through community programs.

Currently exploring roles where I can combine rigorous product thinking with modern AI capabilities to build the next generation of intelligent, user-centric software.

When I'm not shipping, you'll find me tending to my ever-growing collection of houseplants — from trailing pothos to stubborn monsteras. I find the same patience that goes into propagation applies surprisingly well to long roadmaps.
