About Me

Shipping products that matter

A brief look at who I am, where I've been, and how I think about product.

I'm a product leader with deep roots in healthtech, driven by more than just a passion for innovation — I'm motivated by the belief that the right product, built well, can change lives. Having lost a loved one to cancer, I bring a personal sense of purpose to the life sciences space and am committed to helping shape solutions that could spare others from the same loss. While healthtech is close to my heart, I thrive on applying my strengths across industries wherever meaningful problems need solving.

At my core, I'm a connector and a translator — someone who finds energy in bridging the gap between technical and non-technical stakeholders, turning complexity into clarity and friction into flow. I'm drawn to the puzzle-solving nature of product work: using data, pattern recognition, and systems thinking to uncover efficiencies and build smarter, more impactful solutions.

I believe deeply in the power of continuous learning, and I find as much fulfilment in sharing knowledge as I do in acquiring it. Mentoring others and building strong, curious teams isn't just something I do — it's something I lead with.

Currently exploring roles where I can combine rigorous product thinking with modern AI capabilities to build the next generation of intelligent, user-centric software.

When I'm not shipping, you'll find me tending to my ever-growing collection of houseplants — from trailing pothos to stubborn monsteras. Just as I observe how each plant responds to care and adjust my approach, I apply the same iterative mindset to product development: implement, gather feedback, and adapt to deliver the best outcomes.
